Here is a list of the rulers of Dubai from the early 19 th century to the present day, in chronological order:


  1. Sheikh Maktoum bin Butti Al-Maktoum (1833-1852)

  2. Sheikh Saeed bin Butti Al-Maktoum (1852-1859)

  3. Sheikh Hasher bin Maktoum Al-Maktoum (1859-1886)

  4. Sheikh Rashid bin Maktoum Al-Maktoum (1886-1894)

  5. Sheikh Maktoum bin Hasher Al-Maktoum (1894-1906)

  6. Sheikh Butti bin Suhail Al-Maktoum (1906-1912)

  7. Sheikh Saeed bin Maktoum Al-Maktoum II (1912-1958)

  8. Sheikh Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum (1958-1990)

  9. Sheikh Maktoum bin Rashid Al Maktoum (1990-2006)

  10. Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum (2006-present)

Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the current ruler of Dubai, is a highly influential figure who has played a significant role in shaping the city and its development. Here are some interesting facts about him:


  • Visionary Leadership: Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum is known for his visionary leadership and ambition to transform Dubai into a global city and a hub for business, tourism, and innovation.

  • Dubai 2020 Vision: In 2010, Sheikh Mohammed launched the Dubai 2020 Vision, a strategic roadmap that outlined Dubai's goals and aspirations for the future. The vision focuses on creating a sustainable economy, a smart city, a cohesive society, and a vibrant culture.

  • Modernizing Dubai: Sheikh Mohammed has been instrumental in modernizing Dubai's infrastructure and skyline. Under his leadership, iconic structures like the Burj Khalifa, the Dubai Metro, Palm Jumeirah, and Dubai Marina have been developed.

  • Diversification of Economy: Sheikh Mohammed has actively promoted the diversification of Dubai's economy, emphasizing sectors such as finance, real estate, tourism, technology, and renewable energy. He has encouraged the establishment of free zones and provided incentives for foreign investment.

  • Educational Initiatives: Recognizing the importance of education, Sheikh Mohammed has implemented various initiatives to improve the quality of education in Dubai. This includes the establishment of renowned educational institutions like Dubai Knowledge Park, Dubai International Academic City, and the Mohammed bin Rashid School of Government.

  • Humanitarian Efforts: Sheikh Mohammed is known for his philanthropic activities and humanitarian efforts. The Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Global Initiatives, established in 2015, aims to tackle various global challenges such as poverty, education, healthcare, and disaster relief.

  • Author and Poet: Sheikh Mohammed is a prolific author and poet. He has written several books on topics ranging from leadership and governance to poetry and the history of Dubai. His poetry often reflects his deep love for his country and its traditions.

  • Equestrian Passion: Sheikh Mohammed has a keen interest in equestrian sports. He is an accomplished horseman and has been involved in horse racing and breeding. His Godolphin stables are renowned in the horse racing world.

  • Global Recognition: Sheikh Mohammed has received numerous awards and honors for his leadership and contributions to various fields. He is widely respected internationally for his vision, innovation, and commitment to the development of Dubai and the UAE.

  • Social Media Presence: Sheikh Mohammed is active on social media and has a large following. His official Twitter account, @HHShkMohd, has millions of followers and serves as a platform for him to communicate with the public, share insights, and engage in dialogue.
